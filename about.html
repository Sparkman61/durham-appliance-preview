The shop Durham trusts with its appliances.

We're a small, family-run operation — and we like it that way. It's how we keep the quality high, the prices fair, and the promises kept.

For more than a decade, we've done one thing well: fix appliances honestly, and treat people the way we'd want to be treated.

Durham Appliance Thrift & Repair started with a simple frustration — too many repair companies that showed up late, charged for parts you didn't need, and talked down to the people paying them. We thought a local shop could do better by just being straight with folks.

So that's what we built. When you call, you talk to a real person who helps you schedule around your day. When our technician arrives, it's inside a 30-minute window — and he calls before heading over. He'll diagnose the real problem, quote a flat price before touching a thing, and explain what's going on in plain language. If your appliance isn't worth fixing, we'll tell you honestly and point you toward a quality used replacement from our shop instead.

It's not complicated. It's just honest work, done well. And after 155 five-star reviews from our Durham neighbors, we're proud to say it's working.

Whether you need a repair or a reliable used appliance at a fair price, we'd be glad to help. Give us a call — we're easy to talk to.
